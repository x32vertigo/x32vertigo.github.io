Perimeter Institute for Theoretical Physics PSI START

  • In Summer 2022, I participated in the PSI START undergraduate in person research program
  • My primary project focused on the study of asymptotically safe gravity, a theory of quantum gravity that postulates the existence of a non-trivial fixed point in a gravitational renormalization group flow. In particular, the model of interest was that of a homogenous isotropically expanding universe.
  • My secondary project consisted of work in causal dynamical triangulation

Emory University

  • My work culiminated in the creation of a review document, which can be found in the "Projects" section
