University of Toronto Seismic Design Team

  • Since my first year of undergraduate, I have been an active member of UofT's Seismic Design Team
  • Our team's goal is to design a 5 foot Balsa wood tower, which is then tested under seismic load conditions at an international competition
  • My role in the team is to assist the design process by finding ways in which we can optimize the tower
  • One method for generating concepts for structures is performing Topology Optimization using a finite element analysis software called HyperWorks
  • I have also written a genetic algorithm, which searches for the optimal bracing scheme of a truss-element structure, which I hope to make improvements on this year
  • This year, we plan on developing an iterative algorithm that searches for redundant components in our tower's bracing scheme
