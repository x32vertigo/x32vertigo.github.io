Hello! I am a recent graduate of the University of Toronto physical specialist program and incoming Part III student at the University of Cambridge. My current academic and research passions resides in the field of topological quantum matter - the characterization and distinctions of phases of matter via topological properties extending beyond the Ginzberg-Landau paradigm. Additionally, I am interested in the emergent properties of these systems and their possible connections to fundamental physics. Please feel free to take a look at my CV.
